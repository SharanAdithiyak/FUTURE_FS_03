911 Carrera T

Lighter, sportier, manual transmission

The one and always.

The 911 Carrera T: for over 55 years, the T has stood not only for touring, but above all for puristic driving pleasure – thanks to targeted lightweight construction, emotional engine sound, characteristic design elements and precise manual transmission.

Highlights

'T' for Touring

Lighter, sportier, manual transmission: the 911 Carrera T provides even more agility, dynamism and unbridled emotions, especially on winding mountain and country roads.

Refined gearshift lever

The gearshift lever with open-pored plywood ball in walnut and the plaque engraved with 'MT' (Manual Transmission) literally offer an even more pleasant gearshift experience.

Wheels

The 20-/21-inch Carrera S wheels with monochrome crests are high-sheen and painted in Vanadium Grey Metallic as standard or in Gentian Blue Metallic as part of the Carrera T exterior package.

Sports seats Plus

The standard Sports seats Plus (4-way, electric), including seat heating, offer very good lateral support with raised side bolsters and a more pronounced shape in the shoulder area.

GT sports steering wheel

The standard GT sports steering wheel including multi-function has a drive mode switch as standard for choosing different driving modes.

Rear-axle steering

At low speeds, the standard rear-axle steering simplifies manoeuvring or turning and increases agility in tight corners. At higher speeds, it significantly increases driving stability.
