Porsche Experience Programmes

Leave everyday life miles behind you. Behind the wheel of a Porsche, you can take off on legendary race tracks, explore the most beautiful places in the world or enjoy action-packed driving fun on ice slopes as smooth as glass. With the Porsche Experience, you experience your very own personal and individual adventure.

10 Porsche Experience Centres worldwide also offer the opportunity to experience the fascination of Porsche at first hand. Unique worlds of experience fulfil lifelong dreams.

Experience Centers World Wide

Porsche Experience Center Hockenheimring

Since 2019 embedded in the legendary Grand Prix circuit, Hockenheim offers the best conditions with handling tracks, off-road course, dynamic area and slalom area to take your driving fundamentals to the next level.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Leipzig

The 3.7-kilometre FIA-certified circuit consists of 11 bends inspired by the world's most famous race tracks. An ideal training terrain - not only for entry-level formats. Experience innovation at first hand.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Franciacorta

In Italy, there is the first Porsche Experience Center that was certified as a sustainable event location, relying purely on renewable energies. Other highlights: the 2.5-kilometre FIA-approved track and the 1.2-kilometre natural off-road course.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Le Mans

At Le Mans, Porsche created its identity, which is inseparably linked to motor racing. Directly connected to the track of the world-famous 24-hour-race, get the fascinating opportunity to experience a Porsche where it feels most comfortable.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Silverstone

Next to the Formula 1 track, the first Porsche Experience Center of its kind has served as a beacon since 2008. The 6-kilometre FIA-certified track makes it possible to simulate different surfaces: fancy ice and snow in midsummer? No problem.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Shanghai

The first Porsche Experience Center on the Asian continent was built next to the Formula 1 track. With its functional aesthetics, form merges with function. This also applies to the Porsche track, which is divided into 5 different driving areas.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Tokyo

Located in one of Japan's most beautiful areas, the track modules follow the natural topography with differences in altitude for spectacular driving. Moreover, the Tokyo location initiates environmental and educational projects for young people.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Los Angeles

The vibrant city begs to be seen. Since 2016, Porsche fans have been able to try out 9 driving modules to their heart's content – including a replica of the carousel of the Nürburgring Nordschleife and an off-road course with a 40-degree incline.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Atlanta

Opened in 2015, the Porsche Experience Center is also the headquarters of Porsche Cars North America, offering you intense driving experiences. You want to take it easy? View historic vehicles in the Heritage Galley or enjoy sophisticated cuisine.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Toronto (from 2024)

Porsche has always been committed to a unique brand and product experience. In 2024, the tenth PEC will be added in Canada – for the perfect mix of vehicle dynamics, technology and emotion, including a casino, concert hall, luxury hotel and more.

Discover more

Porsche Experience Center Singapore (from 2027)

From adrenaline-filled on-track driving sessions to immersive activities for the whole family. The new PEC Singapore is set to be bring more excitement to the future Changi precinct and aims to be operational in 2027.

Discover more
